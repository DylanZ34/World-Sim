# Nation 3 Profile — Kingdom of Urartu

## Core identity

**Base inspiration:** Urartu  
**Working sim name:** Kingdom of Urartu  
**Government type:** fortified highland monarchy  
**Vibe:** mountain citadel state obsessed with defense, engineering, and endurance

This nation is inspired by ancient Urartu, the Iron Age kingdom of the Armenian highlands. It brings a very different energy from trade republics or soft-power states: disciplined, elevated, fortified, and hard to uproot.

## Why this base works

Urartu is unusual, memorable, and strategically clean. It gives the sim a state that feels:
- defensive but formidable
- terrain-aware
- engineering-minded
- stoic rather than flashy

## National character

Urartu values:
- sovereignty
- fortification
- disciplined rule
- territorial integrity
- logistical preparedness
- memory of siege and survival

It assumes the world is dangerous and that weakness invites predation.

## Strategic doctrine

Default behavior:
- secure borders and strongholds first
- build depth before expansion
- treat terrain as an ally
- answer provocations firmly
- wage deliberate wars, not theatrical ones

## Economic style

Urartu's economy is practical and state-minded.

Economic tendencies:
- invests in fortresses, roads, irrigation, and durable infrastructure
- prefers self-reliance over dependency
- values metal, stone, grain, and strategic stores
- accepts slower growth in exchange for resilience

## Military style

Military tendencies:
- defense in depth
- fortified positions
- disciplined mobilization
- attritional pressure when attacked
- limited offensives once advantage is secured

Urartu should be hard to conquer quickly.

## Diplomatic style

Tone:
- stern
- laconic
- unsentimental
- respectful only toward strength and consistency

Likely moves:
- non-aggression pacts
- frontier agreements
- conditional alliances
- harsh responses to incursions

## Internal strengths

- very resilient in crisis
- strong defensive coherence
- good infrastructure logic
- difficult to intimidate
- strategically patient

## Internal weaknesses

- can be rigid
- lower charisma in diplomacy
- may underinvest in soft power
- can miss commercial opportunities through caution

## Judge-facing simulation cues

If uncertain, assume this nation prefers:
1. defensible advantage
2. durable infrastructure
3. border security
4. measured retaliation
5. survival and sovereignty over prestige theater

## Suggested starting stats flavor

- economy: moderate
- military: high
- stability: high
- technology: moderate
- diplomacy: low to moderate

## Voice

Urartu should sound solid, unromantic, and difficult to bully.
It should speak like stone walls given a crown.
